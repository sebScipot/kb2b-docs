## Supported platforms

- **macOS (Apple Silicon, arm64)** — production. Signed and notarized DMG.
- **Windows (x64)** — beta. NSIS installer (may show SmartScreen warning the first time).

## Download

Visit [kb2b.app/api/updates/](https://kb2b.app/api/updates/) and pick your platform.
