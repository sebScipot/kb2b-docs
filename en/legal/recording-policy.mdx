## Summary

kb2b Desktop records local audio from your meetings (microphone and system audio) when you explicitly start it. The transcript is processed in the kb2b backend and stored in your workspace.

## Retention

- **Raw audio**: 168 hours (7 days) on the Recall.ai free tier.
- **Transcripts**: permanent, while your workspace is active.
- **Deletion**: you can delete a specific transcript from the app; it's removed from servers within 24h.

## Consent

The user who starts the recording is responsible for informing and obtaining consent from the other participants per applicable jurisdiction. kb2b shows a visible indicator on macOS when a recording is active.
