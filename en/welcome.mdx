## Every account has a POT. Every POT has memory.

kb2b is a B2B knowledge base where each customer account lives as a **POT** (Knowledge Pot) — a specialized container holding every document, meeting transcript, and fact extracted automatically. You ask. kb2b answers with the source and its **POT Score** (the confidence metric, 0.0–1.0).

kb2b is built on top of [SciPot](https://docs.scipot.ai), the certified memory infrastructure for AI agents. SciPot provides the POTs and POT Scores; kb2b provides the use experience tuned for commercial teams.

**A concrete example:** you ask kb2b _"When does the contract with this account expire?"_. kb2b answers with the date, points you to the source document `framework-contract-2026.pdf` (section 3.2), shows you the POT Score for that fact is `0.92 — Verified` (validated by a Human Curator on your team), and reminds you of the last meeting recorded with the account — all in one response. Every teammate sees the same thing. No answer ships without its source.

## What kb2b is not

- **Not a generic chatbot.** It does not live on the open internet. It lives inside your accounts and your documents.
- **Not just another meeting recorder.** The recording is local, bot-free, and the transcripts feed the account's memory.
- **Not a CRM.** It does not replace Salesforce or HubSpot. It coexists — kb2b is the narrative brain, the CRM is the structure.
