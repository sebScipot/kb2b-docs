## Warnings and blocks (on the real token limit)

- At **95% of your token limit**, you see a yellow warning in kb2b. You can still query.
- At **110% of your token limit**, queries are blocked. You need a top-up, plan change, or to wait for next cycle. Details on [Token limits](/en/admin/token-limits).

Meeting minutes have their own independent counter, with dedicated warnings and top-ups.
